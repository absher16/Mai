Mai Is About You Telling Your Story

For social media storytellers working hard to create engaging content everyday, Mai is here to help!

Mai uses Google Vision tools and Natural Language Processing to automatically generate text content to match your photos. Put more simply, you give Mai a photo, and Mai will find the words to complete the narrative. Mai will then publish the blog post in a crisp format for your readers to enjoy.

Let's start telling your story today!
